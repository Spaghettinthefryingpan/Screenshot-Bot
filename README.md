# Screenshot-Bot
A bot I made take screenshots of stuff and to make into a pdf

READ ME!

I made this to download Raws for scanlations use this how you want. As long as if it's not child porn I do not condone my work being used for exploitation. :D

I would recommend to have your browser in full-screen it's better I personally used Firefox.

Don't move your mouse while it's taking screenshots nothing bad will happen the process stops though.

Feel free to change the settings around just mess around until it looks good the default settings are just what work on my laptop.

The files usually go into a temp folder so just search around until you see "shots" then everything should be there.
