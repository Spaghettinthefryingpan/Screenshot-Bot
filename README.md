# Screenshot-Bot
This A bot I made to take screenshots of stuff and to make into a pdf

READ ME!

I made this to download Raws for scanlations

I would recommend to have your browser in full-screen it's better but the auto cropping usually does it's job but it sometimes fails especially when it's confusing I personally used Firefox.

Feel free to change the settings around just mess around until it looks good the default settings are just what work on my laptop.

The files usually go into a temp folder so just search around until you see "shots" then everything should be there. Or wherever you decide to put it.

Also If Smart Control Is being annoying just paste in this in your command prompt change the path to wherever you have the .exe
